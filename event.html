It is a war based crisis stimulation event occurring in the forthcoming world. The Artemis Galaxy is divided into four supreme planets: Cybertron, Krypton, Worlorn , Coruscant. Each team would be a minor planet in one of these Jovian planets and would be randomly assigned in one of these.
